Title: A brief Introduction to zeta
Speaker: Wang Xiyuan
Time: Saturday, March 10, 2012, 13:30--16:30
Venue: CMS 203

Abstract

It is commonly believed that the zeta function is at the center of modern mathematics. It is an important research object as well as an important tool associated with various branches of mathematics.

My report will introduce several zeta functions, including their com- mon properties, their respective characteristics and their connection with other branches of mathematics. The focus of my report is on explain- ing some well-known theorems, conjectures, theories and important ideas. There is not require much prerequisits. I will try my best to make everyone understand my report.
